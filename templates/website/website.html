Welcome to My World

About Me

Hello, my name is Denny Lu. I am a passionate photographer residing in the picturesque city of Irvine, California. I grew up in Temecula and graduated from Great Oak High School with a stellar GPA of 4.33, ready to conquer the world with my lens. I then pursued my studies at UC Irvine, a decision which further fueled my desire to capture the beauty that surrounds me.

My journey as a photographer is complemented by my experience in leadership and community service. I am a proud recipient of the Gold Presidential Service Award and a Congressional Service Award, both evidences of my commitment towards community service and leadership beyond the lens.

My Work

My work as a photographer is an ever-evolving journey in the pursuit of excellence. Over the years, I have honed my skills through various projects and collaborations that have tested my creative prowess and technical expertise.

Each frame captured tells a unique tale, a blend of vivid colors and emotions that invite viewers into a story out of everyday moments. My portfolio showcases various projects, ranging from serene landscapes to intimate portraits, each with a distinctive narrative. I aim to not just capture moments, but to create lasting impressions that resonate with audiences and leave a lasting imprint on their minds.

My Education

As a cornerstone of my professional foundation, my education has played a crucial role in shaping my journey as a creative professional. I graduated from Great Oak High School with a remarkable GPA of 4.33, setting a strong academic basis for future pursuits.

Following high school, I attended UC Irvine, a prestigious institution where I deepened my knowledge and honed my skills. During this time, I balanced rigorous academic pursuits with my passion for photography, participating in numerous projects that bridged theoretical learning with practical applications, paving the way for my current professional endeavors.
